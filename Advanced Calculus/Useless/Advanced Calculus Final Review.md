## 2.3 ~ 2.4
> [!note] Bolzano-Weierstrass Theorem (波爾查諾-魏爾斯特拉斯定理)
> Every bounded sequence in $\mathbb{R}$ has a convergent subsequence.

> [!Tip] Definition (Cauchy)
> A sequence $\{x_n\}$ in $\mathbb{R}$ is a ==Cauchy sequence== if $\forall \varepsilon>0$, $\exists N\in\mathbb{N}$ such that $|x_n-x_m|<\varepsilon$ as $n,\ m\ge N$.

> [!Note] Theorem (柯西數列必收斂)
> Let $\{x_n\}$ be a sequence in $\mathbb{R}$. 
> Then $\{x_n\}$ is a Cauchy sequence $\iff$ $\{x_n\}$ is a convergent sequence.
